Introduction

Who are we?

Welcome to MetroFit NYC, more than just a gym - it's an epitome of a fitness culture in New York City. Located in the bustling heart of NYC, we are an oasis for urban dwellers seeking a healthy lifestyle and vigorous workout routine. Our mission is to inspire community members to tap into their inner strength through our state-of-the-art facilities, comprehensive training programs, and personalized fitness experiences.

MetroFit NYC was established in 2002 by a group of fitness enthusiasts who are passionate about healthy living. We believe that fitness should be a lifestyle, not just a part of your daily routine. Every day, we help our members overcome barriers, reach personal goals, and develop sustainable living habits with our guidance
